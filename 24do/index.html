Straight-to-the-point task writing

Writing down your to-dos are as simple as it gets.

Write down what you have to do

We specifically made it easy for you to write down your tasks. We've used other apps and they're filled with categories and settings. We said NO, it should be as simple as 1-2. And it is.

Choose a time to be reminded

Sometimes you know you're gonna be busy in the afternoon and might forget to do that one thing. Setting a reminder before getting busy will help you remember because let's face it, we have too much to focus on.

Get reminded of your important tasks

Next time you check your phone, you might just remember something important.

Notifications all the way

We will send you a notification to remind you of the things you need to do. Don't worry, it's not an alarm that will go off whether you are at a meeting or somewhere else. Next time you pick up your phone, you should see it right there on the screen.

Check it off or leave it for later

Once you get reminded, you can choose to either be done with the reminder or set it for later. Sometimes you just have to snooze.
