About

Each and every day, we are reminded of our differences. We are continually spurred by the media and politicians to be outraged by what the “others” are doing, why they are reprehensible, and why they should be shunned.

We feel that there is another way.

We feel that celebrating the things we have in common with the people around us can foster a sense of curiosity and lead us to learn more about each other. We feel that utilizing information technology to connect strangers who happen to be in the same vicinity and share common interests can lead people to have interesting conversations that bridge the gaps over their differences.
